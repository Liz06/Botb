FAQ - At Festival

How Much Alcohol Can I Bring In With Me ? -

Each person over the age of 18 may bring up to 20 x 500ml cans with them OR 7 litres of cider/lager/beer. Due to safety and laws around our site no glass is allowed, so we ask that all alcohol is brought in plastic bottles or cans, with the seal unopened, so we can ensure the safety of our guests. This will be checked upon entry by our security guards who will be manning the doors.

When Can I Arrive On Site ? -

Gates open at 7am on Saturday 30th July 2018 and close at 12:30pm. Bands will start to perform from 1:30pm, so this enables people to have time to get ready. On saturday and sunday the gates are open from 7am until 12:30pm. If guests want to leave later in the day we will then open the gates from 9-11pm, so people can leave. If you want to come on the friday this is posible, IF YOU BUY A EARLY ENTRY PASS, as well as your tickets. For early entry the gates will be open from 12:30pm, until 10pm - if you want to know more about the early entry pass, check out the FAQ-Tickets page.

When Can I Stay On Site Till ? -

Guests are welcome to stay on site until 12:30pm on Monday the 2nd of july, but please ensure you are all packed up and ready to leave by then. As children will have school the next day, so we need to ensure that the site is completely clear.

What Should I Do If I Spot A Fire ? -

If you discover a fire please raise the nearest alarm and inform a steward or security guard. DO NOT attempt to deal with the fire yourself, but follow signs to the nearest fire point, making sure you/others stay safe in the process.
