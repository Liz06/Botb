FAQ - Travel

What Is The Best Way To Get To Venue ? -

There are many different accessible routes which can be used to get to Battle Of The Bands. We recommend using public transport where possible

If I Am Getting The Train What Is The Best Way To Get To The Festival From The Station ? -

For the festival we run a regular shuttle bus, going from Harrogate Train station to Harrogate Grammar School, for free if u can show us your tickets to Battle Of The Bands. If you park in the centre or outskirts of harrogate, you can also catch this bus, to reduce traffic and clutter around the venue. This Service runs every 20 mins from 7am, until 11pm.

Why Are There No Transport Or Coach Tickets Available On Your Site ? -

We don't offer any travel tickets as our company doesn't offer any buses or coaches, due to the widespread of people attending the festival it would be too expensive, and we wouldn't be able to meet everyone's needs, so we made the decision not to offer this service and ask everyone to make their own travel arrangements. If you get a taxi we do provide a discount code, that if shown to driver you will get 30% off your fair, as we know travel is expensive.
