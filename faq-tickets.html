FAQ - Tickets

When Will I Receive My Tickets ? -

Tickets will be emailed or posted to the recipient, 1 month before the event.

If I Can’t Come Anymore Can I Get A Refund ? -

If you can no longer attaned the festvial, you can email our team and get a full refund on your ticket, up to 5 days prior to the event. If on the day you cannot attened the festival for unforseen circumstances, you can also email our team, stating your reason and you will recieve a refund.

What does the early entry pass enable you to ? -

If you purchase the early entry pass along side your tickets this allows you to arrive at the festvial on the friday instead of coming after 9am on the Saturday. The gates will be open until 10:30pm on the friday for people to check in. This early entry pass includes free camping friday night ONLY. Tickets for the festival need to be purchased seperately.

I haven’t recieved my booking confirmation, what do i do ? -

All booking confirmations are sent to the ticket buyers email. If you have not recieved your confirmation, the most common reason is you typed your email in incorrectly on your orde.
