Things To Do Around Event -

There are a number of different things to do in and around harrogate. If you are looking for a place to eat or an activity to do you will find this in harrogate, it has a large range of restaurants from the ivy, to mcdonalds, you could also catch a film at the brand new everyman cinema.

Directions -

The venue for battle of the bands is easily accessible, if you type the location of Arthurs Ave, Harrogate HG2 0DZ, into the google maps below this will give you clear directions on how to reach the venue from your location.

Parking At Venue -

There is limited parking at our venue, with one main car park, which holds approximately 100 cars, then roadside parking, which you can use if there is space, although it is residents parking, so it would be advised that you walk or use public transport where possible, to reduce congestion and ensure you get into the venue in time.

Disabled Access -

There is disabled access throughout the venue, but there is also numerous amounts of stairs inside the building which are not suitable for wheel chair users, so you would need to follow the signs for the disabled access, to ensure you don't get stuck inside the venue, you can easily get from the from to the back of the building leading to outside, so there is no need to let this be an issue.
