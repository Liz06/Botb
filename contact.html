FAQ'S

What Is Battle Of The Bands ? -

Battle Of The Bands is a 2 day festival located at Harrogate Grammar School in Harrogate UK. Battle of the bands has a variety of artists and groups performing throughout, who strive to provide you with the best entertainment.

Can I Attend The Festival If Im Under 18 ? -

Our event welcomes families bringing teens or children with them, although all teens must be accompanied by a adult for the duration of the festival. Due to their being alcohol on the site, when the children come through the doors showing their ticket, they will receive a blue wristband, this is to ensure safety. Unaccompanied teens cannot attend this festival.

Is boomtown suitable, if i have accessibility requirements ? -

Our festival has provisions in place for disabled access, please see our information page for more details. Please keep in mind hat our site contains hills and grass as its outdoors, so certain parts may be inaccessible
